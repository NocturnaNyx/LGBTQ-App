Gender Identities

Cisgender

A term used to describe people whose gender identity aligns with the sex they were assigned at birth.

Gender Fluid

A term referring to a person who does not identify with any fixed gender.

Gender Flux

A term used to describe a person whose gender identity fluctuates in intensity.

Transgender

An often misconceived umbrella term that refers to people whose gender identities do not match the sex they were assigned at birth.

Sexual Orientations

Asexual

An umbrella term that describes people who do not experience sexual attraction to others.

Pansexual

A term used to describe a person who is sexually attracted to others of any gender identity.

Romantic Preferences

Polyamorous

A term used to describe a person who desires a relationship containing more than the traditionally accepted two members. Polyamory is often misconstrued as being unfaithful to one’s partner, however this is false as polyamorous relationships occur with the full consent of all involved parties.

Panromantic

A term used to describe a person who is romantically attracted to others of any gender identity.

Miscellaneous Terms

Gender Dysphoria

A term used to describe the psychological and emotional conflict that a nonbinary person feels in relation to differences between their physical sex and their gender identity. Gender dysphoria can be extremely distressing and cause lasting psychological damage to nonbinary and transgender people.

Gender Non-Conforming

A term used to describe those whose behaviours and appearance do not align with societal norms regarding to their gender identity.

Misgendering

A term used to describe the use of a pronoun or word in regards to a person that does not align with the person’s gender identity.

Questioning

A term used to describe a person who is unsure of their gender identity, sexual or romantic orientations.
